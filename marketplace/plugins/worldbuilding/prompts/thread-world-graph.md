Thread the world graph for {{scope|the current worldbuilding encyclopedia}}. Find entries with few or no [[links]], add backlinks where relationships are already established, and suggest 5–10 high-value links that would make characters, settings, factions, themes, and lore easier to navigate. Do not invent new canon to justify a link.
